Pizza

Description

A delicious pesto Pizza

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Pesto Sauce
  • Fresh Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Roll out Dough
  2. Spread Sauce onto Dough
  3. Put cheese ontop of sauce and dough
  4. Spread basi ontop for garnish
  5. Cook @ 425 for 10 minutes or until Golden Brown
