Burger

Description

A delicious juicy burger

Ingredients

  • Burger Bun
  • Burger
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Grill buns
  2. Cook Burger on grill until medium
  3. Put cheese on the burger patty while it has about 1 minute left to cook so cheese can melt.
  4. Assemble burger on bun and add desired condiments
